# Nykaa Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI project analyzes Nykaa's sales performance, profitability, customer demographics, and product return trends. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and profit performance
- Identify high-performing cities and customer segments
- Evaluate product return trends
- Measure profit margin and business growth
- Support strategic business decisions through interactive dashboards

---

## 📊 Key KPIs

- 💰 Total Sales: ₹5M+
- 📈 Total Profit: ₹1.15M
- 📊 Profit Margin: 22.3%
- 👥 Highest Revenue Age Group: 18–25
- 📍 Top Performing City: Delhi
- 🔄 Return Rate: 10%

---

## 🛠 Tools & Skills Used

- Power BI
- Power Query
- Data Modeling
- DAX
- KPI Design
- Data Visualization
- Business Intelligence

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Delhi generated the highest sales revenue.
- Customers aged 18–25 contributed the highest revenue.
- The business achieved a profit margin of 22.3%.
- Product returns accounted for approximately 10% of total sales.
- Interactive visuals help identify sales trends and profitability across different customer segments.
